Pan Fried Onion Cheeseburger

Description

I make these burgers with caramelized onions and sharp Cheddar cheese. I serve them on brioche buns and eat them plain.

Ingredrients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80 percent lean)
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons salted butter
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 slices sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 3 rolls brioche, split and toasted

Steps to Make

  1. Place ground beef in a bowl and season with Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly and shape into 3 balls. Set aside.
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet. Add onion and cook until onions start to brown, 5 to 7 minutes. Remove from the skillet. Add 3 ground beef balls to the same skillet and flatten with a cast iron press or a heavy spatula. Evenly top each patty with onions and cook for 3 to 4 minutes more.
  3. Carefully flip patties over so that the onions are now on the bottom, pressing once again with cast iron press or heavy spatula. Top each with a slice of cheese and cook until cheese is melted and burgers have reached the desired doneness, 3 to 4 minutes. Serve on brioche buns.
